Rod of Absorption

Rod, very rare (requires attunement)

While holding this rod, you can use your reaction to absorb a psi that is targeting only you and not with an area of effect. The absorbed psi power's effect is canceled, and the psi power's energy--not the psi itself--is stored in the rod. The energy has the same level as the psi when it was cast. The rod can absorb and store up to 50 levels of energy over the course of its existence. Once the rod absorbs 50 levels of energy, it can't absorb more. If you are targeted by a psi that the rod can't store, the rod has no effect on that psi.
When you become attuned to the rod, you know how many levels of energy the rod has absorbed over the course of its existence, and how many levels of psi energy it currently has stored.
If you are a psicaster holding the rod, you can convert energy stored in it into psi slots to cast psi powers you have prepared or know. You can create psi slots only of a level equal to or lower than your own psi slots, up to a maximum of 5th level. You use the stored levels in place of your slots, but otherwise cast the psi as normal. For example, you can use 3 levels stored in the rod as a 3rd-level psi slot.
A newly found rod has 1d10 levels of psi energy stored in it already. A rod that can no longer absorb psi energy and has no energy remaining becomes nonpsionic.
