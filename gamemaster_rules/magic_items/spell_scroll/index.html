psi Scroll

Scroll, varies

A psi scroll bears the words of a single psi, written in a mystical cipher. If the psi is on your class's psi list, you can use an action to read the scroll and cast its psi without having to provide any of the psi's components. Otherwise, the scroll is unintelligible.
If the psi is on your class's psi list but of a higher level than you can normally cast, you must make an ability check using your psicasting ability to determine whether you cast it successfully. The DC equals 10 + the psi's level. On a failed check, the psi disappears from the scroll with no other effect.
Once the psi is cast, the words on the scroll fade, and the scroll itself crumbles to dust.
The level of the psi on the scroll determines the psi's saving throw DC and attack bonus, as well as the scroll's rarity, as shown in the psi Scroll table.

A Technomancer psi on a psi scroll can be copied just as psis in psibooks can be copied. When a psi is copied from a psi scroll, the copier must succeed on an Intelligence (Arcana) check with a DC equal to 10 + the psi's level. If the check succeeds, the psi is successfully copied. Whether the check succeeds or fails, the psi scroll is destroyed.
